About me

My background is in Optimization, Machine Learning, and Signal Processing.

My current research focuses on the theory and applications of Optimization and Machine Learning to networked systems such as Internet-of-Things.

I am looking for self-motivated students and visitors working in the areas of machine learning, optimization, and networked systems.
Please drop me an email if you are interested.
